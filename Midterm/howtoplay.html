Let's Go on an Adventure!

Account and Login


Previous Next

Create an account and login. Make sure you are using the correct server.

Layout




This is the layout of the screen.
On the top left, there is the Paimon Menu button, Map, Elemental Sight button, Co-Op Mode button, and Quests button.
On the top right, there is the Events button, Battle Pass, Wish button, Adventurer Handbook, and Character button.
On the bottom left, there is a Chat function. There are also invisible controls to move your character in the desired direction.
On the bottom right are more controls: jumping, running, Normal Attack, Elemental Skill, and Elemental Burst.

Paimon Menu




The Paimon Menu is the pause menu. It is unlocked after the Archon Quest, "Unexpected." The Paimon Menu cannot be accessed in Domains and Spiral Abyss.

Quests




In the Quests tab, you can see all the quests you need to complete. You can press the "Navigate" button to get directions on what to do or where to go for the quest.

Katheryne




Katheryne is the receptionist for the Adventurers' Guild. You can speak to her to claim the Daily Commission Reward, to claim Adventure Rank Rewards, or to dispatch characters on expeditions. There are multiple Katherynes in each of the nations of Teyvat.

Map


Previous Next

This is the map of Teyvat. Currently, you can only unlock Mondstadt, Liyue, Inazuma, Enkanomiya, The Chasm, Sumeru, and the Serenitea Pot. You can use this map to teleport to different locations that you have unlocked.
If you zoom in, you will notice more details and symbols. If you zoom out, you can see your progress for exploring the area.
If you click on the button on the bottom right, you can open the map menu of locations.

Teleports


Previous Next

The Statue of The Seven are structures found throughout Teyvat. They are used for unlocking regions when interacted with, teleporting, automatic and manual healing, changing the Traveler's element, offering rewards in exchange for Oculi, and a warmth source in Subzero Climate.
Teleport Waypoints are also found throughout Teyvat. They are used for teleporting, respawing location after your character party dies, warmth source in Subzero Climate, energy recharge for the Lumenstone Adjuvant, and reducing Decay in Withering Zones.

Character


Previous Next

In the Character tab, you can view details about all the characters you have, such as its attributes, weapons, artifacts, constellations, talents, and profile.
In the attributes section, you can level up your character, customize your character, and view your character stats.
In the weapons section, you can change the weapon your character is using and enhance and refine weapons.
In the artifacts section, you can equip and enhance artifacts to your character to make them stronger.
In the constellations section, you can view and unlock constellations for your character. You can obstain constellations by getting duplicates of a character you already have. Each character has six constellation levels.
In the talents section, you can level up your character's talent level to enhance their skills.
In the profile section, you can view details about your character, such as their birthday, voice actor, vision, and more.

Party Setup




The Party Setup tab is where you pick what characters you want in your party. You can only have up to four characters in your party at a time. You will unlock the ability to change characters at Adventure Rank 2.

Profile


Previous Next

The Profile is a customizable display that can be viewed by other players. It includes your UID, Nickname, Character Avatar, Namecard Banner, Adventure Rank, World Level, Signature, Achievements Progress, Spiral Abyss Progress, Character Showcase, and Namecards. You can edit which characters and Namecards you would like to show.

Inventory


Previous Next

The Inventory is where you can view all the items you have obtained in your journey through Teyvat. You can view your weapons, artifacts, character development items, food, materials, gadgets, quest items, precious items, and furnishings. You can also view how many Primogems and Mora you have.

Adventurer Handbook


Previous Next

The Adventurer Handbook has many useful features.
The Experience tab offers rewards based on your progress.
You can also selected your preferred region to receive Daily Commissions in the Commissions tab.
The Domains tab displays the domains you have access to.
You can display and track the locations of enemies and possible drops with the Enemies tab.
The Guide tab recommends priority quests to be completed.
You can get rewards from the Embattle tab by ascending characters and completing Elemental Trial.

Wish




The Wish tab is where you can view the available banners and wish for the character(s) and/or weapon(s) you want. You can also see how much Masterless Starglitter, Masterless Stardust, Primogem, Intertwined Fate, and Acquaint Fate you have.

Battle Pass




The Battle Pass is a reoccurring event where you can complete daily, weekly, and BP Period missions in order to gain Battle Pass EXP and get rewards. There are 50 levels total. You could also purchase the Gnostic Hymm or Gnostic Chorus packages to obtain extra rewards. The Battle Pass is unlocked at Adventure Rank 20.

Events




The Events tab is where you can view the current events that are happening. Be sure to complete these before time is up because these events have a limited amount of time to complete and some have limited time rewards.

Cooking


Previous Next

In Genshin Impact, you can cook food for your characters to consume. Different foods can give your characters different types of buffs. To cook food, you will need to have enough ingredients. You can also cook ingredients.

Crafting


Previous Next

In Genshin Impact, you can craft materials. When ascending a character or weapon, it will require certain types of materials. If you do not have enough, you can craft them with drops you have obtained.
You can convert Character Ascension Material of a certain element to another element.
You can also use the Mystic Offering to create an artifact with three other artifacts.

Fishing


Previous Next

In Genshin Impact, you can go fishing for fish. If you see circular rippling waters, it is a fishing spot. You can use different rods for certain buffs when fishing. You also need different Bait to catch different types of fish. Once a fish bites, you have to control the line's tension to reel in the fish.

Forging




In Genshin Impact, you can forge items, such as enhancement ore, weapons, and gadgets.

Weapons


Previous Next

When you view a weapon, you can see the details, enhance the weapon, or refine the weapon. Enhancing the weapon will require enhancement ore and mora. Refining the weapon will require duplicates of the same weapon and mora.

Serenitea Pot


Previous Next

The Serenitea Pot is a system that allows you to create your own home. You can enter your Serenitea Pot with a gadget and visit your friends' Serenitea Pots in Co-Op Mode regardless of World Level. Inside the Serenitea Pot, there is the Teapot Spirit, Tubby.

Friends




In Genshin Impact, you can add other players to your Friends List. Adding friends makes it easier to check their Profile, join their world for Co-Op sessions, and visit their Serenitea Pot. You unlock the Friends List at Adventure Rank 2. You unlock Co-Op Mode at Adventure Rank 16.
